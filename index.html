Intro

After working as a corporate drone in business development, I realized that I wanted a job that would encourage me to test the limits of my knowledge and push me to learn everyday. With the ever-evolving landascape of the internet, I discovered that web development would be the best route for me to weave creativity and learning together. Having had experience using SquareSpace, Wix, etc. - all sites with pre-designed templates - I saw how important it was to have customizable and personalized websites to showcase a business, product, idea and more.

I have only been a student of Lambda for less than two weeks, but the amount of information that I have added to my arsenal has exponentially grown. Lambda continues to test me and question my drive to succeed. Along this journey, I hope to add to my portfolio and put out projects that are inherently me by design.
